Rock Paper Scissors

How to Play

Rock Paper Scissors is a simple hand game played between two players. Each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes: rock, paper, or scissors.

  • Rock beats scissors (rock crushes scissors).
  • Scissors beats paper (scissors cut paper).
  • Paper beats rock (paper covers rock).
